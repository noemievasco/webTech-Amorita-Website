A M O R I T A

Puerto Princesa, Palawan Pangiao, Boko

Amorita Resort is a luxury boutique resort located on the tranquil eastern end of Alona Beach in Panglao Island, Bohol, Philippines. Perched atop a limestone cliff, it offers breathtaking views of the Bohol Sea and direct access to the white-sand beach below.

With elegantly designed suites, exclusive pool villas, and premium amenities, Amorita Resorts caters to discerning travelers seeking both relaxation and escape. Indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments, take a dip in stunning infinity pools, or savor exquisite dining experiences that celebrate local and international flavors.

Activities @ Amenities

Archery

Enjoy some fun and excitement by aiming the shot at our free archery sessions. Whether you’re a virtuoso or just trying to learn how to shoot a bow and arrow, sharpen your focus by shooting and firing with an entertaining way to spend the day. This activity is also open to young children, provided an adult accompanies them.

Watersports

Paddle through the crystal clear waters of Alona Beach or find your balance on one of the stand-up paddle boards. Available every day.
